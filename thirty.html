Dirty Thirty


They say your inhibitions loosen as you age, but as many of you know, mine weren't all that strong to begin with. So despite turning thirty, I'll try to keep it locked up for one night only!

This party is loosely open-invite: bring your friends, but not their friends. We have front/back porches/yards, featuring a fire pit and a patio heater, and we'll keep a cooler of beer stocked outside, so you can chill outside if you're extra concerned about COVID (friendly reminder that the updated boosters are now available!).

For drinks, we're keeping it simple: we'll have snacks and some beer/cider/wine/liquor/non-alcoholic stuff, and for my birthday, nothing would make me happier than---you guessed it---more beer! Or snacks!
