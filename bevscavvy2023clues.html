Bev Scavvy 2023 Clues

  • Snap a team selfie with Seattle's O.G. rock star. Have your team captain send it to Sheridan in a Signal direct message (DM).
  • Score a drink from a vending machine (it goes without saying that someone must chug it immediately). Send a video of the successful vend and chug to the Signal group.
  • This pillar of Seattle industry leaves his massive stones on display for all to contemplate and caress. Your captain will Signal DM Sheridan a picture of your team doing so.
  • Play a round of pinball. Send a pic of your team with their (hopefully, but not necessarily, high) score to the Signal group.
  • This Seattleite has blood on his hand--but no matter your party affiliation, you've gotta take a team selfie with him and have your captain Signal DM it to Sheridan!
  • Scavvers, sate yourselves with something scrumptious! Not just any food will do, though--you must eat (or get takeout) from somewhere with exactly 4.5 stars on Yelp. If some of your team already ate or has onerous dietary restrictions, worry not--just spend $20 or more, and have your captain send the Signal group a picture/screenshot of the receipt, and a screenshot of the Yelp page.
  • Where once there was death, one might now find Irish Death; where once dwelt the Holy Spirit, there now dwells Holy Mountain. Takest thou a selfie on mine steppes and DMest it thou to Sheridan on Signal via thy captain.
  • Everyone's got that Dawg in 'em, but it can be hard to find at times. Have your captain send Sheridan a Signal DM that describes what is bundled to the grundle of UW Basketball's bronze mascot.
  • Maybe you can't find your inner Dawg--but that's okay, in Seattle, there are Dawgs everywhere! Find one and take a selfie with them (with their knowledge and consent, of course) and their student ID for proof. Have your team captain send the selfie to the Signal group. You may not share the same Dawg with another team.
  • We forgot to buy prizes for the top finishers! Fortunately, you can help. Buy a 22oz or 750ml bottle of beer made by a WA brewery and bring it with you to the finish.
