Bev Scavvy 2024 Clues

The main prize goes to all teams arriving at the finish location before 8:30pm. There are also three secondary prizes! Clues 5, 10, and 11 each afford you an opportunity to earn "points," and the team with the most points wins a prize---regardless of whether they completed all clues successfully. Clues 4 and 12 are photo contests, to be soberly and impartially judged by the Committee at 9pm sharp, whose winners will each also win a prize.
  1. Go where patrons walk softly but carry big paddles; win a point and take a page from beneath paddles' pips.
  2. You probably won't find another 45lb catfish, but there should be plenty of interesting microörganisms in the water sample you bring us from the Emerald City's shittiest lake. Hope you remembered your water bottle (or other container)!
  3. These immobile commuters have been waiting 85 years for the next streetcar to arrive. Just like local residents, cheer them up by donning them in an article of clothing.
  4. This local Fortune 500 establishment's founder came to Seattle in search of gold, but found drip instead. Send your most stylish teammate to their dressing rooms and send the Signal group a photo. Prize for best photo.
  5. A great scav team embraces risk. Share a meal together, earning the following points based on the food safety rating of the establishment you patronize: Excellent = 1, Good = 2, Okay = 3, Needs to Improve = 5. Bring a receipt.
  6. How many bikes went across the Fremont Bridge so far today? Prove it.
  7. Take the one true path to the center of Seattle, the single greatest way to travel. Avoid licking any interior fixtures during your ride so as to not contract the Epstein-Barr virus.
  8. It's surprisingly easy to find in a haystack and can withstand a 9.0 earthquake---but can it withstand the gentle caress of your tongue? Send photo.
  9. Buy a tale with a worn spine where tails are pet and spines are arched.
  10. Seattleites love eating a bag of these. Bring back a receipt from at least one; earn a point for each unique location's receipt you bring.
  11. Graffiti your team's initials on the interior of the cold half of a Cold Snack. Earn a bonus point for slappin' a sticker up there too. Photo.
  12. Find your döggelganger on the streets of Seattle, and take a photo of one of your teammates with them---on all fours, of course. Prize for closest resemblance.
  13. Bring a 4- or 6-pack of an alcoholic beverage whose single-decimal ABV multiplied by ten is prime.
